Webinar on Studies in Europe

❮ ❯

As a Silver Jubilee Alumni Event, IEEE UVCE organized a Webinar on Studies in Europe and Germany, presented by Dr. Vandana Jha. The speaker is a qualified Postdoctoral in CSE and currently working as a Senior Scientist at KIT Karlsruhe. The main aim of this webinar was to provide valuable insights into higher education in Europe, giving exposure to high quality education with diverse programs, research opportunities and also strong professional networks with career opportunities for engineering students.

After welcoming everyone, the speaker provided an overview of masters in Europe and focused specifically in Germany. The outline from the speaker was how to apply for scholarships, application for Masters program and other application processes.

The speaker gave information on eligibility for higher studies and grades of language which are useful for basic communication. The speaker suggested reference links for scholarship and courses that align with academic and career goals. Lastly there was a Q&A session where the participants clarified doubts on specific concerns about the preparation.

Date: 31st August, 2024
Time: 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM
Platform: Google Meet
No. of Participants: 59
