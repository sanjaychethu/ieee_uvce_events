IEEE UVCE x Samsung Innovation Campus

❮ ❯

IEEE UVCE in association with Samsung Innovation Campus, conducted a month-long Python coding and programming workshop. The hybrid workshop was divided into three batches with dedicated instructors.

Participants progressed from Python fundamentals to object-oriented programming, receiving guidance and support from instructors to overcome challenges. The workshop culminated in a written assessment and a three-day hackathon.

Student teams tackled real-world problem statements, showcasing their projects to a panel of judges. Teams that successfully presented in hackathon and those who achieved 90% assessment score, were awarded certificates of completion in a ceremony attended by esteemed dignitaries, including the CEO of ESSCI.

All participants received a Samsung kit that included a bag, water bottle, and T-shirt.

Dates: 3rd June to 12th July, 2024
Venue: LH Complex and Senate Hall
No. of Participants: 150
