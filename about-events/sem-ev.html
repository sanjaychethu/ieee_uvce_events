Seminar on EVs and Hybrid Vehicles

❮ ❯

The seminar provided insights into EVs and Hybrid Vehicles with a focus on evolving technology and sustainability. It explored the latest developments in the EV industry and offered a look at the future of mobility.

Dr. Sanjeev K Nayak, EDU Manager at Alstom with 18 years of experience in EV product development and testing, led the seminar. He covered essential topics like Battery Management Systems (BMS), Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), and power electronics in EVs.

The session also touched on DC-DC converters for energy management and the integration of Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) for improved safety. Dr. Nayak briefly explained the role of aerodynamics and vehicle dynamics in enhancing vehicle efficiency.

Participants gained practical knowledge about the EV industry and its sustainable future. The event encouraged discussions on innovation and engaged all attendees.

Date: 21st September, 2024
Time: 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM
Venue: Senate Hall, UVCE
No. of Participants: 150
