Voltorb - August 2024

Voltorb is a monthly contest where a given problem statement has to be solved by building a feasible solution in the form of an electronic circuit, either by simulation or a physical circuit.
For this month's Voltorb, participants were asked to develop an automated system using Arduino for managing irrigation and pest control. The system should be able to use real-time data to optimize water usage, reduce pesticide use, and promote sustainable crop growth.

Winners
1st Place: Team Ohmies, 2nd year ECE
2nd Place: Team SVPS, 2nd year ECE
3rd Place: Kannika K R, 2nd year ECE

Platform: Online
Date: 15th to 30th July, 2024


CAD-A-THON August 2024

The CAD-A-THON event held in August 2024 focused on tackling a complex engineering design challenge. Participants were tasked with devising innovative solutions through their proficiency in CAD software, applying mechanical engineering concepts, and optimizing their designs.
The competition emphasized the importance of creating effective and feasible designs that met the specified requirements.
