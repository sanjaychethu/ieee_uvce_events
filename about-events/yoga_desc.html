International Day of Yoga

❮ ❯

IEEE UVCE hosted The International Day of Yoga celebration at UVCE. The event aimed to promote the physical, mental, social, and spiritual benefits of yoga and raise awareness about these benefits. Dr. Kiran K, the Branch Counselor of IEEE UVCE, served as the yoga instructor, and the Principal, Dr. Paul S Vizhian, participated in the session.

In preparation for the event, a circular was sent to all students, informing them about the International Day of Yoga celebration and encouraging their participation and breakfast was provided after the yoga session.

Date: 21st June, 2024
Time: 6:30 AM - 8:30 AM
Venue: Quadrangle, UVCE
No. of Participants: 35
