Roadmap to UPSC
Session 2

❮ ❯

IEEE UVCE hosted the second session of "RoadMap to UPSC", a workshop held as part of the Silver Jubilee Alumni events. The second session was held online and had good interaction and provided a valuable learning experience to the participants.

The Speaker Smt. Priyanka Srinivasamurthy, Dy. Secretary & Dy. Financial Advisor (Dy. FA) at the Office of Special Director General, South Zone, Hyderabad, Central Reserve Police Force, Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt. of India, was also a member of ICAS Batch of 2012 and Chairperson of IEEE UVCE in 2006. The speaker shared her valuable insights to the aspiring civil servants in UVCE.

The session provided a detailed strategy for preparation, emphasizing the significance of various aspects like essay writing, self-study, and staying informed about current affairs. Overall, both sessions served as the perfect guideline towards civil services.

Date: 6th July, 2024
Time: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Platform: Google Meet
No. of participants: 137
