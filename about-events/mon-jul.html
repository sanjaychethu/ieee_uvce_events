Voltorb - July 2024

Voltorb is a monthly contest where a given problem statement has to be solved by building a feasible solution in the form of an electronic circuit, either by simulation or a physical circuit.
For this month's Voltorb, participants were asked to develop a Smart Pet Feeder incorporating IR sensors to detect a pet's presence and automatically dispense food. They were asked to devise a method for portion control and scheduled feeding, and include an LCD for real-time monitoring.
