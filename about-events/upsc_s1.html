Session 1

❮ ❯

IEEE UVCE organized an insightful Silver Jubilee Alumni event, "Roadmap to UPSC". This session was held offline. This workshop aimed at guiding civil service aspirants.

The keynote speaker was Ms. Hamsa Shree N A, an esteemed alumna from the CSE batch of 2021 and former Vice Chairperson of IEEE UVCE, who secured a UPSC rank of 866 in 2024. Ms. Hamsa Shree N A, shared her journey from engineering at UVCE to civil service, discussing the structure, eligibility, and importance of UPSC, along with the challenges and strategies that worked for her.

The event concluded with a Q&A session, providing an interactive platform for participants to engage directly with the speaker.

Date: 5th July, 2024
Time: 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM
Venue: Senate Hall
No. of Participants: 137
