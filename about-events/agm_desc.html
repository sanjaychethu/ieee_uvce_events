Annual General Meet
2024-25

❮ ❯

The IEEE UVCE Annual General Meet 2024 marked a significant milestone as IEEE UVCE enters it's 25th year.

The event commenced with warm greetings extended to all teachers and attendees, followed by an invocation song and the ceremonial lighting of the lamp by esteemed dignitaries. Dr. Kiran K sir, Branch Counselor, IEEE UVCE, presented the Annual Report of the year 2023-24, highlighting all the events organized by Team IEEE UVCE and the commendable achievements attained during the period.

Dr. P Deepa Shenoy ma'am, Branch Advisor, IEEE UVCE, unveiled the special logo marking the 25th anniversary of IEEE UVCE and announced the new team for the year 2024-25. Further, the batch of 2023-24 were felicitated for their contributions.

The meet concluded with a vote of thanks extended to all teachers and students for their valuable presence and participation.

Date: 24th May, 2024
Time: 2:30 PM
Venue: Sir M Visvesvaraya Senate Hall
No. of participants: 124
