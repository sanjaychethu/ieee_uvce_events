UDAAN

❮ ❯

IEEE UVCE AESS conducted a techno-managerial flagship event ‘Udaan’ with the aim to develop the students entrepreneur capabilities along with the creative touch of paper planes. The event was exclusive for 1st year students.

Participants were provided with points to make paper planes, making deals to secure the highest returns. The time of flight was taken into account, and paper planes were tested for their performance, adding a practical and competitive edge to the event. Visiting all sectors and making strategic deals earned participants rewards and developed a sense of strategic thinking. Bonus points were awarded for answering aviation-related questions, making the event both educational and engaging. The top deal-makers and trivia champions who showcased their business acumen and aviation knowledge were rewarded.
