All General Societies & SIGs Monthly

General

AGM 2024-25 24th May, 2024

The IEEE UVCE Annual General Meet 2024 marked a significant milestone as IEEE UVCE enters it's 25th year. The event commenced with warm greetings extended to all teachers and attendees, followed by an invocation song and the ceremonial lighting of the lamp by esteemed dignitaries. Dr. Kiran K sir, Branch Counselor, IEEE UVCE, presented the Annual Report of the year 2023-24, highlighting all the events organized by Team IEEE UVCE and the commendable achievements attained during the period.

General

International Day of Yoga 21st June, 2024

IEEE UVCE hosted The International Day of Yoga celebration at UVCE. The event aimed to promote the physical, mental, social, and spiritual benefits of yoga and raise awareness about these benefits. Dr. Kiran K, the Branch Counselor of IEEE UVCE, served as the yoga instructor, and the Principal, Dr. Paul S Vizhian, participated in the session.

Societies

PELS Day Webinar 21st June, 2024

IEEE UVCE PELS chapter hosted a PELS Day webinar with Ms. Rinika Paul. Her talk explored the fundamentals of Power Electronics, showcasing its role in miniaturizing devices (like those with Taiwan Semiconductors' 1.6nm tech). Different semiconductor devices were discussed, including the rising star Gallium Nitride (GaN).

General

Webinar on Studies in Europe 31st August, 2024

As a Silver Jubilee Alumni Event, IEEE UVCE organized a Webinar on Studies in Europe and Germany, presented by Dr. Vandana Jha. The speaker is a qualified Postdoctoral in CSE and currently working as a Senior Scientist at KIT Karlsruhe. The main aim of this webinar was to provide valuable insights into higher education in Europe, giving exposure to high quality education with diverse programs, research opportunities and also strong professional networks with career opportunities for engineering students.
